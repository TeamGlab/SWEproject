ABOUT US

Our primary focus is representing UF as we compete in SAISA and ICSA regattas around Florida and the Southeastern US. We also encourage both beginner and leisure sailors to come out to Lake Wauburg with the team as we host Learn to Sails and Leisure sailing events! Feel free to reach out and we'd love to help you get shredding out on the water!

View UF Club Sailing scores: https://scores.collegesailing.org/schools/florida
