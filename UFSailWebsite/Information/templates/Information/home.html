ABOUT US

The UF Sailing Team is a 100% student-run club sailing team that races at both the regional and national level. UF's competitive sailing team also collaborates with leisure sailors. The purpose of this association shall be to encourage, teach, and advance sailing and racing among students and faculty: and to promote the sport and its high ideals in every possible way, both at the University of Florida and elsewhere.
